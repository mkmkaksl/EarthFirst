Electric Vehicle (EV) Charging Stations

  • Some stations can be integrated with solar panels for cleaner charging
  • Installation costs depend on the type and capacity of the charging station
  • Some governments offer incentives for EV charging infrastructure
  • Encourages the adoption of electric vehicles, reducing carbon emissions

Community Solar Programs

  • Community solar programs allow homeowners to invest in or subscribe to a shared solar energy system
  • Participants receive credits on their energy bills based on their share of the system's output
  • Ongoing benefits include reduced energy bills
  • Enables access to solar energy without the need for rooftop installations

Energy-Efficient Lighting

  • LED and CFL bulbs are more energy-efficient than traditional incandescent bulbs
  • While initial costs may be higher, the long lifespan and energy savings make them cost-effective
  • Reduces electricity consumption and the need for frequent bulb replacements

Solar Ventilation

  • Solar-powered ventilation systems use sunlight to power fans or vents that improve air circulation
  • Improves indoor air quality using renewable energy and reduce the need for traditional electrical ventilation
  • Potential long-term energy savings from solar panels

Home Energy Monitoring Systems

  • Smart home energy monitoring systems track energy usage in real-time
  • They provide insights to help homeowners make informed decisions about energy consumption
  • Savings through better energy management
  • Promotes energy awareness and efficiency

Green Roof Systems

  • Provide insulation, reduce stormwater runoff, and improve air quality
  • Enhances building insulation, reducing energy needs for heating and cooling
