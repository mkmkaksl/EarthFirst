Average Households

The average household consumes ~800-1,000 Kilowatt-hours(kWh) of electricity per month. This equates to ~9,600-12,000 kWh annualy!

Around The World

The world uses ~180,000 terwatt-hours(tWh) of energy, with more than 75% of energy coming from fossil fuels!

Renewable Energy

Albania, Paraguay, Ethiopia, & Nepal produced more than 99.7% of their electricity using renewable energy sources in 2022. This is a great achievement and scientists describe it as a "irreversible tipping point"
